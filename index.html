Company Overview and Assessment Context

EWM Global is a 24-year-old fintech C-corporation specializing in deferred cash and coinvestment plan administration for financial services firms. Operating from Stamford, CT with 85 employees across 3 locations, the company generates $24M in annual revenue with 15% growth while targeting 20% expansion. This comprehensive assessment evaluated 12 critical business functions to identify strengths, risks, and opportunities for achieving sustainable growth in a competitive landscape dominated by Computershare, GPx, and HRSoft.
